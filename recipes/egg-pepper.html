Egg in a Pepper

Description

This recipe is paleo and a super easy way to eat your eggs! Can also top with cheese or even a slice of bread.

Ingredients

  • 1 large egg
  • 1 (1/4 inch thick) ring bell pepper
  • Salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat.
  2. Place bell pepper ring in the hot skillet.
  3. Crack egg into bell pepper ring; cook until bottom holds together and corners are browned, 2 to 3 minutes.
  4. Flip and cook until desired doneness is reached, 2 to 3 minutes more; season with salt and ground black pepper.
